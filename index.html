MATHEMATICS

Mathematics is the basis of everything including software development. A good percentage of programming languages depend on Mathematics as a basis for their structure. This shows that Mathematics is key in our daily lives.

THE BUILDING BLOCKS OF MATHEMATICS

Mathematics is divided into three main parts. These are:

  • Pure Mathematics
  • Applied Mathematics
  • Statistics

Pure Mathematics

This is basically the theory of Mathematics. Most people shy away from this branch of Mathematics because it can be a bit challenging. it includes content like:

  • Discrete Mathematics
  • Linear Algebra
  • Number Theory
  • Real Analysis
It defines Mathematics in detail and why we accept it as it is. It's the reasoning behind Mathematics.

Applied Mathematics

This branch defines the applications of Mathematics in our day-to-day lives. It is a bit fun because this is where most calculations and formulas appear. It includes subjects like:

  1. Fluid Mechanics
  2. Differential Geometry
  3. Partial Differential Equations
  4. Ordinary Differential Equations
  5. Analytical Applied Mathematics

Statistics

This is where the Analytical part of Mathematics comes in. Statistics is very broad. It includes aspects like:

  • Regression Modelling
  • Probability and Statistics
  • Design and Analysis of Sample Surveys
  • Theory of Estimation
  • Test of Hypothesis
